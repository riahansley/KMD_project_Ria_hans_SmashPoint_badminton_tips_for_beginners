Badminton

Badminton is a lightning-fast racket sport where speed, strategy, and precision rule. Players use smashes, drops, and drives to outplay opponents. Popular in Asia and beyond, it’s a top Olympic and professional sport.

Facts about Badminton:

  • Badminton is played on a rectangular court that’s 13.4 meters long and 6.1 meters wide for doubles.
  • The shuttlecock has 16 feathers, usually from a goose or duck
  • Footwork is crucial, and professional players can cover the court in just a few steps.
  • The longest recorded badminton rally lasted over 2 hours.
  • Some professional shuttlecocks are handmade and cost more than $50 each.

In short, badminton is a fast and dynamic game that challenges both the body and mind. People everywhere play it for fun, competition, and to stay fit while improving agility and quick thinking.
